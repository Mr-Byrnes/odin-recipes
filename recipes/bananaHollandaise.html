Ham and Bananas Hollandaise

Ingredients:

  • Bunch of Bananas
  • Canned Ham
  • 4 eggs
  • Cheese

Steps:

  1. Peel all the bananas and place them in a lasagna tray.
  2. Open can of ham and placed thinly cut strips on top of the bananas.
  3. Crack 4 eggs and scramble on medium heat on a skillet.
  4. Add cheese to the eggs and remove while still liquid.
  5. Do whatever else is required to make a hollandaise sauce from what was done.
  6. Pour hollandaise over the bananas and ham and bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
