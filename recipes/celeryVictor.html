Celery Victor

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch of celery
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 beef bouillon cube
  • 1/4 cup low calorie Italian salad dressing
  • Pimiento strips

Steps:

  1. Trim root end off celery but do not separate stalks.
  2. Remove leaves and coarse outer stalks.
  3. Cut celery bunch crosswise once so bottom section is 5 inches long.
  4. Cut bottom section crosswise into quarter; tie quarters with string.
  5. In skillet, heat water to boiling; dissolve bouillon cube in water.
  6. Add celery bundles.
  7. Cover; heat to boiling. Cook about 15 minutes.
  8. Drain celery; place in shallow glass dish.
  9. Pour salad dressing over celery. Refrigerate 3 hours, turning bundles twice.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
