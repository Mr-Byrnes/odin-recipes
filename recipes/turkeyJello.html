Ham and Bananas Hollandaise

Ingredients:

  • Fresh canned Turkey
  • Frozen Peas
  • Frozen Carrots
  • Frozen Peas
  • Frozen Spinach
  • Gravy falvoured Jell-O

Steps:

  1. Cook the vegies by boiling them and letting them cool
  2. Using the gravy from the canned turkey, create the best tasting turkey gravy Jell-o.
  3. Grab your favorite Jell-o salad bowl and add a little bit of Jell-O to the bottom.
  4. Layer in the carrots at the bottom, followed by the peas, spinach and tuekey.
  5. Fill the rest of the bowl with the rest of the Jell-O and let rest for 6 hours in the fridge.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
